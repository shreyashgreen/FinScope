🎯 KEY FUNCTIONS IMPLEMENTED

1. INDUSTRY_DOMAIN_MAP (Dictionary - Data Structure)
   ├─ Technology (5 industries): Consumer Electronics, Software, Semiconductors...
   ├─ Finance (3 industries): Banks, Insurance, Financial Services
   ├─ Manufacturing (3 industries): Auto, Electric Vehicles, Industrial
   ├─ Consumer (3 industries): Retail, Specialty, Apparel
   ├─ Energy & Materials (3 industries): Oil, Chemicals, Metals
   ├─ Healthcare (3 industries): Pharma, Biotech, Medical Devices
   ├─ Consumer Goods (3 industries): Beverages, Food, Household Care
   └─ Media & Entertainment (2 industries): Entertainment, Broadcasting

2. fetch_peer_data(tickers: List[str]) → pd.DataFrame
   • Fetches metrics from yfinance for multiple tickers
   • Returns: Ticker, Name, Currency, Price, Market Cap, Revenue, 
             Div Yield, P/E Ratio, Domain, Industry
   • Sorting: By Domain (alphabetical), then Market Cap (descending)
   • Caching: 3600 seconds (1 hour)

3. get_intelligent_peers(ticker, industry, sector, market_cap, country)
   • Finds similar companies using market cap similarity (0.1x - 10x range)
   • Geographic weighting (same country gets 1.3x multiplier)
   • Domain-aware organization
   • Returns: List of up to 6 peer ticker symbols

4. fetch_enhanced_news(ticker, company_name=None) → List[dict]
   • Fetches from Yahoo Finance exclusively
   • Each item includes: title, summary, link, sentiment, category
   • Sentiment: positive | negative | neutral
   • Categories: Earnings, Mergers, Regulatory, Product, Market, Executive,
                Economic, General
   • Caching: 600 seconds (10 minutes)
